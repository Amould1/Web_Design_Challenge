Summary: Latitude v. Humidity

The objective of this plot/visualization was to show whether or not there was a correlation between the level of humidity in a city and the latitude of that particular city. Based on the data and made clear by the visualization there is no evidence to clearly prove the coorelation between latitude and humidity. The data shows no pattern because of the scattered nature of all of the data points.
